Summer, the season of Blueberries

Matching their name, blueberries love blue skies! In the first months of summer, they start off slow with only about 5% of the annual harvest ripening early on. But as summer hits its stride, the harvest skyrockets to around 30% during the peak months, before gently tapering off to about 5% again as autumn rolls in.
